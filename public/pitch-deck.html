HiveLink

Family care coordination — the nerve centre for every care circle. S$0–39.90/month.

  • 🏠 Family Dashboard — real-time status for every family member worldwide
  • 💊 Smart Medication — visual reminders, refill tracking, drug interaction alerts
  • 🆘 SOS Emergency — one-tap with smart escalation & fall detection
  • 📱 Elderly Tablet UI — 24pt+ fonts, 3-tap max, ambient photo frame
  • 👩‍⚕️ Helper Hub — task dashboard, care logging, multi-language translation
  • 🤖 AI Insights — anomaly detection, cognitive decline indicators, burnout alerts
  • 📄 Document Vault — medical records, prescriptions, insurance in one place
  • 🌐 7+ Languages — EN, CN, Malay, Tamil, Bahasa, Thai, Tagalog

HiveOS

B2B SaaS for 200+ Senior Activity Centres & Active Ageing Centres. S$200–800/month per centre.

  • Health Vitals Logging — BP, glucose, weight with trend alerts
  • Automated AIC Reporting — subsidy reports in minutes, not days
  • Family Visibility Portal — parents see SAC activities & vitals
  • Staff Dashboard — scheduling, volunteer mgmt, incident reporting
